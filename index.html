"The future of E-cars in India...!!!"

Despite what many may believe, the future of electric vehicles in India is incredibly bright. Thanks to the advent of path-breaking EV technologies in India as well as the world and the willingness to share these technologies for the global good has led to low manufacturing and driving costs. Not only have electric vehicles (EVs) proven to be a cleaner alternative to fossil-fuel-powered vehicles, but they are also one of the most cost-effective ones. This is a huge plus for EV adoption in India, given the soaring fuel prices. The strong initiatives taken by the Indian government for EV adoption deserve special mention here. Through tax subsidies and stricter regulations, the government is encouraging newer potential vehicle owners to opt for an EV rather than a fossil-fuel car. Add to it; many credible lending institutions are extending easy car loans to make EV purchases possible. As a result, the end consumer is able to purchase electric vehicles in exchange for a significant tax rebate and finds it relatively cheap to drive them.
